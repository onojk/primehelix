Title: On the Distribution of Smaller Factors in Semiprimes
Author: Jonathan Kendall
Date: April 24, 2026

This submission contains:
- paper.tex (LaTeX source)
- paper.pdf (compiled output)

The paper studies the distribution of semiprimes n = pq using the logarithmic coordinate
theta = log p / log n, presenting empirical evidence for a scaling law
P(theta_0) ≈ C / theta_0 (as N → ∞) and demonstrating that several geometric and spectral
embeddings fail to capture this structure.
